What Is Claims Repricing?

Claims repricing determines what a health plan should pay for a medical service based on a contracted or reference fee schedule — rather than the provider's billed charges. It's the foundation of accurate claims adjudication, network adequacy analysis, and provider contract negotiation.

Cloud Health Office gives you direct access to the same CMS fee schedule data used by Medicare Administrative Contractors, so you can verify rates before paying a claim, audit existing payments, or benchmark your commercial contracts against Medicare.

Supported Fee Schedules

All three major CMS payment methodologies, updated for 2025.

RBRVS

Physician Fee Schedule
  • Work, PE & MP RVU components
  • Geographic (GPCI) adjustment
  • Facility / non-facility rates
  • Conversion factor applied
  • Modifier adjustments (50, 52, 62, 80)

OPPS

Outpatient Prospective Payment
  • APC assignment & payment rate
  • Status indicator logic
  • Device-intensive add-ons
  • Composite APC grouping
  • Multiple procedure discounting

MS-DRG

Inpatient Prospective Payment
  • DRG relative weight
  • Hospital base rate
  • Wage index adjustment
  • Outlier threshold
  • Transfer policy logic
